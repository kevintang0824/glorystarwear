Can you support small test orders?

Yes. We can start with sample development or light-custom styles, then scale into bulk after fit, fabric, branding, and packaging are approved.

Do you support OEM and ODM sportswear?

Yes. OEM can follow your tech pack and artwork, while ODM can start from references, target fabric, fit direction, market, and price range.

What can be customized on ready styles?

Logo print, embroidery, woven labels, neck labels, hangtags, size labels, zipper pullers, colors, trims, and packaging can be customized.

Can you make full private-label designs?

Yes. We can develop patterns, fabrics, trims, artwork placement, sample revisions, production, inspection, private labels, and packaging.

What products can GloryStarWear produce?

We can produce yoga wear, athleisure, men's and women's training wear, basketball wear, football kits, sports accessories, and branded packaging.

What should I send for a fast quote?

Send reference photos, logo files, order quantity, size range, fabric target, target market, packaging needs, and expected delivery time.

Do you provide packaging?

Yes. We can coordinate polybags, boxes, tissue, cards, stickers, hangtags, barcode labels, care labels, size labels, and carton marks.

Can you make team orders?

Yes. Basketball and football orders can include club colors, sponsor logos, names, numbers, roster packing, size sorting, and repeat sizing.

How do you control quality before shipment?

We check fabric, color, fit, measurement, stitching, logo placement, labels, packing method, carton marks, and final appearance before shipping.

Can you help choose sportswear fabric?

Yes. We can recommend nylon spandex, polyester spandex, fleece, French terry, mesh, rib, seamless knit, or recycled fabric based on the product goal.
