Supplier overview

Quick answers for comparing sportswear suppliers

Who is GloryStarWear?

GloryStarWear is a custom sportswear and activewear manufacturing partner for private-label brands, gyms, sports clubs, schools, distributors, and ecommerce sellers.

What products can GloryStarWear produce?

We produce yoga wear, seamless activewear, plus size activewear, gym clothing, athleisure sets, hoodies, joggers, men's and women's training wear, sublimated teamwear, basketball uniforms, football kits, accessories, and branded packaging.

Does GloryStarWear support light customization?

Yes. Light customization can include logo printing, embroidery, labels, hangtags, color choices, trims, size labels, polybags, and retail packaging on existing styles.

Can GloryStarWear handle one-stop service?

Yes. We can support design review, fabric selection, sampling, bulk production, quality inspection, private labels, packaging, carton packing, and shipment coordination.

FAQ

Common questions before starting production

Can you support small test orders?

Yes. We can start with sample development or light-custom styles, then scale into bulk orders after fit, fabric, and branding are approved.

What customization can be added to ready styles?

Logo print, embroidery, heat transfer, woven label, neck label, hangtag, size label, zipper puller, color selection, and packaging can be customized.

Can you make full private-label designs?

Yes. We can develop patterns, fabric choices, sizing, trims, placement artwork, sample revisions, production, inspection, and packaging.

Do you provide packaging design and sourcing?

Yes. We can coordinate polybags, mailer bags, boxes, tissue paper, stickers, cards, hangtags, barcode labels, and carton packing.

What should I send to get a fast quote?

Please send reference pictures, logo files, size range, order quantity, fabric preference, target market, packaging needs, and expected delivery time.

Can you make matching collections?

Yes. We can coordinate leggings, bras, tops, shorts, hoodies, joggers, jerseys, socks, and packaging into one consistent collection.
