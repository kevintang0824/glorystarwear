Direct answers

Quality questions buyers ask before ordering

How does GloryStarWear control sportswear quality?

We check fabric, color, fit, measurements, stitching, logo placement, label content, packing method, carton marks, and final appearance before shipment.

Can you inspect logo and label details?

Yes. Heat transfer, embroidery, sublimation, woven labels, neck labels, hangtags, barcode labels, and polybags can be checked against approved samples.

Do you support size tolerance checks?

Yes. We compare bulk garments with the approved size chart and tolerance rules before final packing.

What should buyers approve before bulk production?

Approve fabric, color, fit sample, logo execution, label artwork, packaging, size chart, and carton packing rules before bulk cutting.

Need a clear QC plan?

Share your product category, expected order quantity, size range, and inspection requirements.
