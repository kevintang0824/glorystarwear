Process answers

What buyers need to know before production

Clear inputs reduce sampling time, prevent production mistakes, and help both sides confirm the right route for custom activewear, teamwear, and packaging.

What information is needed for a quote?

Product category, reference images, quantity, logo files, size range, fabric target, customization method, packaging needs, and delivery market.

When does bulk production start?

Bulk starts after sample, fabric, size chart, logo execution, label artwork, packaging, and payment terms are approved.

Can the process support OEM and ODM?

Yes. OEM can follow your tech pack, while ODM can develop from references, target price, market, fabric direction, and brand requirements.

How is shipment prepared?

We plan carton marks, SKU sorting, size ratio packing, packing lists, export documents, and forwarder coordination before goods leave production.

Want to start from a simple brief?

Send whatever you have now. References and quantity are enough for the first production route discussion.
