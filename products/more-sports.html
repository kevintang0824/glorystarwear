Can GloryStarWear make niche sportswear categories?

Yes. GloryStarWear supports OEM and ODM development for running wear, tennis apparel, pickleball apparel, cycling wear, volleyball uniforms, golf apparel, baseball teamwear, swimwear, combat sports apparel, compression layers, and outdoor training pieces.

Can these products use custom logos and labels?

Yes. Logo printing, heat transfer, embroidery, woven labels, neck labels, hangtags, size stickers, polybags, and retail packaging can be customized.

Are team and club orders supported?

Yes. We can sort by player, size, number, colorway, club, or event, and prepare cartons for teams, schools, leagues, and distributors.

Can I combine multiple sports into one order?

Yes. Buyers can combine apparel categories into a single program, such as running tops, compression layers, socks, caps, and branded packaging.
