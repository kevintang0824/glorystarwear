What to confirm for joggers and tracksuits

Joggers and tracksuit programs need clear decisions on fit, fabric stretch, waistband comfort, pocket utility, cuff shape, zipper quality, and SKU sorting.

Fabric

Fleece, Terry, or Nylon

Choose fleece for lifestyle, French terry for daily wear, interlock for warmups, or stretch woven nylon for track pants.

Fit

Tapered or Relaxed

Confirm inseam, rise, thigh width, cuff opening, pocket depth, waistband height, and jacket fit.

Trim

Drawcords and Zippers

Customize drawcords, zipper pulls, pocket zippers, side stripes, reflective tape, rib cuffs, and woven patches.

Pack

Set or Separate SKU

Pack as joggers only, tracksuit set, team warmup set, size ratio carton, ecommerce SKU, or player roster pack.
