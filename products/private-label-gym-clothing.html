How to brief private label gym clothing

A clear gym clothing inquiry should define product mix, fabric purpose, fit direction, logo method, label system, packaging, quantity, and delivery market.

Product Mix

Tops, Bottoms, Layers

List tees, tanks, pump covers, shorts, joggers, hoodies, leggings, socks, caps, and bags.

Performance

Sweat, Stretch, and Fit

Choose moisture-wicking, compression, breathable mesh, cotton handfeel, or heavyweight fleece.

Brand System

Logo, Labels, Hangtags

Prepare vector logos, placement notes, neck labels, woven labels, care labels, hangtags, and stickers.

Retail Prep

Packaging and SKU Sorting

Plan polybags, size stickers, barcode labels, set packing, carton marks, and ecommerce prep rules.
