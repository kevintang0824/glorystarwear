Buyer pathways

Turn visual references into a production-ready brief

A strong inquiry includes category, target market, quantity, fabric direction, logo method, size range, packaging needs, and delivery country.

For activewear brands

Start with yoga wear, training sets, athleisure, compression layers, color cards, fabric handfeel, and label system.

For teams and clubs

Prepare roster details, team colors, crest files, sponsor marks, names, numbers, socks, and player packing rules.

For ecommerce sellers

Use packaging, barcode labels, size stickers, hangtags, SKU sorting, polybags, and carton plans from the beginning.

For distributors

Plan repeatable size ratios, neutral colorways, reorder styles, carton marks, warehouse requirements, and delivery support.
