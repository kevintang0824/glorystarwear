Can triathlon suits be customized?

Yes. Tri suits, two-piece race sets, race jerseys, compression shorts, sleeves, swim caps, labels, colors, and packaging can be customized.

What fabrics are used for endurance apparel?

Common options include quick-dry jersey, aero mesh, compression spandex, lightweight woven panels, and smooth low-friction fabrics.

Can race event packing be supported?

Yes. We can prepare size sorting, athlete packs, barcode labels, event cartons, sponsor marks, and accessory bundles.
