Customization focus

What to decide for running tops

Running top buyers usually compare breathability, weight, seam comfort, visibility, club color, and how the product will be packed for teams or events.

Fabric

Quick-Dry Mesh

Choose lightweight polyester mesh, bird-eye knit, or stretch jersey based on race or training use.

Safety

Reflective Trim

Add reflective tape or print at shoulder, back, hem, or side panel for night running visibility.

Branding

Heat Transfer Logo

Use durable logo transfer, neck label, size label, hangtag, and club color matching.

Packing

Event Ready

Pack by runner, team, size ratio, retail SKU, or race kit with barcode and carton labels.
