What to confirm for hoodie samples

Hoodie quality depends on fabric weight, shrinkage, print durability, hood shape, cuff recovery, seam stability, and the packaging system.

Fabric

Fleece or French Terry

Choose brushed fleece, French terry, heavyweight fleece, cotton blend, or performance fleece by season and target price.

Fit

Oversized or Athletic

Confirm body length, shoulder drop, sleeve width, hem width, hood shape, pocket depth, and size grading.

Decoration

Embroidery and Print

Use embroidery, puff print, screen print, applique, woven patch, rubber patch, or heat transfer logo.

Retail

Labels and Packaging

Prepare woven neck labels, care labels, hangtags, size stickers, polybags, barcode labels, and carton marks.
