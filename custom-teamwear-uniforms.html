A custom teamwear uniform manufacturer produces branded sports uniforms for clubs, schools, leagues, gyms, distributors, and teams, including fabric choice, size grading, artwork placement, logo method, player names, numbers, quality control, packing, and delivery support.

Buyer FAQ

Custom teamwear uniform questions

Can GloryStarWear make team uniforms with names and numbers?

Yes. We can add player names, player numbers, sponsor logos, club crests, sleeve marks, and size labels based on the roster and artwork files you provide.

What team sports can be produced?

We can support basketball, football, volleyball, baseball, softball, rugby, hockey, youth sportswear, training wear, warmups, fan merchandise, and related club apparel.

Can you pack uniforms by player or team roster?

Yes. We can pack by player, by team, by size, by SKU, by carton, or by delivery group, with barcode labels, size stickers, and carton marks when needed.

Can sponsor logos and club crests be added?

Yes. Sponsor logos and club crests can be produced by sublimation, heat transfer, embroidery, woven badge, silicone patch, or mixed methods depending on the design and fabric.

What should teams send for a fast quote?

Please send sport category, product list, quantity by size, artwork, club colors, logo files, name and number list, fabric preference, packaging needs, and delivery country.
