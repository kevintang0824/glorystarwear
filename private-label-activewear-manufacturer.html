What does a private label activewear manufacturer do?

A private label activewear manufacturer helps brands produce apparel under their own label, including product development, fabric selection, sampling, logo application, size grading, bulk production, quality control, and packaging.

Buyer FAQ

Private label activewear questions

Can GloryStarWear make activewear under my own brand?

Yes. GloryStarWear can produce activewear with your logo, labels, hangtags, packaging, colors, fabric direction, and product specifications.

What activewear products can be developed?

Common products include leggings, sports bras, crop tops, tanks, biker shorts, gym tees, compression layers, hoodies, joggers, tracksuits, and matching sets.

Can I start without a complete tech pack?

Yes. You can start from reference images, target fabric, size range, logo files, quantity, and packaging needs. We can help organize the sample direction.

Can labels and packaging be customized?

Yes. We support woven labels, care labels, neck labels, hangtags, size stickers, barcode labels, polybags, boxes, and carton labels.

What is the difference between OEM and ODM activewear?

OEM follows your existing tech pack and artwork. ODM starts from references and development goals, then helps shape product mix, fabric, fit, trims, and packaging.
